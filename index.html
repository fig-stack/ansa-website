How it works

From call to confirmed
in under a minute.

Your customers call your number. Everything else is handled.

Step 01
📞

Customer Calls

Your customer calls your business number. Ansa picks up on the first ring, 24/7 — no voicemail, no hold music, no missed opportunities.

Step 02
📅

Ansa Books It

The AI receptionist has a natural conversation, checks your Google Calendar for real availability, and locks in the appointment — all in real time.

Step 03
✉️

Confirmation Sent

The moment the booking is confirmed, the customer gets a text with their appointment details. The event appears on your calendar instantly.

desk does. Automated.

🕐

24/7 Availability

Ansa never sleeps, never calls in sick, and never puts a customer on hold. Every call is answered immediately, even at 11pm on a Sunday.

Live integration with Google Calendar means Ansa checks actual availability and creates the event the second the booking is confirmed.
