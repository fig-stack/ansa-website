How it works

Your phone gets answered.
Your calendar gets filled.

You don't change anything. Ansa plugs into your existing number and calendar and handles the rest.

Step 01

Your phone rings

A customer calls your number. Ansa picks up on the first ring, every single time — no voicemail, no hold music, no missed opportunity.

Step 02

Ansa handles it

It has a natural back-and-forth with your customer, checks your real calendar for open slots, and locks in the booking — just like a person would.

Step 03

Done. On to the next.

Your customer gets a confirmation text. The appointment appears on your Google Calendar. You never had to stop what you were doing.

Features

Everything a great receptionist does.
Without the overhead.

Ansa handles the full front-desk workflow so you can stay focused on your actual work.

Picks up every time

No call goes to voicemail. No customer gets put on hold. Ansa answers immediately — at 8am, 8pm, or midnight on a Sunday.

Books on your real calendar

Live Google Calendar integration. Ansa only offers slots that are actually open, and creates the event the moment the booking is confirmed.

Texts the confirmation

Every booking triggers an instant text — date, time, service, location. Your customer has it before they hang up.

Handles cancellations too

Customers can call to cancel or reschedule. Ansa finds the appointment, confirms the change, and updates your calendar. No back-and-forth needed.

Sounds like a real person

Not robotic, not scripted. Ansa has a warm, natural conversation. Most callers have no idea they're talking to an AI.

Live in 48 hours

No hardware, no IT team, no complicated setup. We configure everything. Most clients are live within two days of signing.
